Traditional dance club

The traditional club teachers students cultures of both Rwanda and Burundi. It teaches traditional dances andplaying drums. The club organizes performances during school events and cultural festivals, fostering a sense of pride and appreciation for our rich heritage. Through this club, students not only learn about their own culture but also gain a deeper understanding of the diverse traditions that make up our community.
