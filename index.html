Welcome to Cornerstone Leadership Academy Rwanda

Advancing structured leadership, governance excellence, and disciplined student engagement within a formal institutional framework.

Vision

"We seek to create a life transforming learning environment that will mold young men and women into future leaders for Rwanda... men and women whose lives fully reflect the character qualities and leadership principles embodied in the life of Jesus."


Mission

"To build visionary Leaders through integrity and excellence."


Goal

"To mold young men and women into people who are physically, mentally, socially, and spiritually fit to gear up the transformation process in the society."




Key commitment areas

  • Discipleship and character development
  • Servant Leadership mentoring
  • Unity across diversity
  • Academic excellence through hard work and continuous improvement
  • priority on long term relationships

Core Values

  • Being Jesus-Christ centered
  • Integrity
  • Stewardship
  • Forgiveness and reconciliation
  • Appreciating diversity
  • Love and care
  • Respect and humility
  • Responsibility



Students' Departments

School Council

Oversees student governance, supervises ministries, ensures student leadership excellence.

Leadership Practice Team

Organizes debates, public speaking sessions, morning fellowships, and MUN to train future leaders.

Church Ministry

Includes Mass Choir, Intercession Group, and Evangelism Team, fostering spiritual growth.

Caregroups

Supports student welfare, mentorship, and personal development.
